Background

About Me

I am an Electrical and Electronics Engineering student with a strong focus on embedded systems, hardware design, and real-world engineering. My work centers on building functional hardware projects — from programmable macropads and autonomous drones to wireless communication systems and custom IoT devices.

I have hands-on experience with microcontrollers including Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi Pico, along with protocols such as I2C, UART, SPI, and ESP-NOW. I design circuits, write firmware in C and Python, and debug hardware at the component level.

I approach engineering with a focus on reliability, clean design, and practical functionality. I am seeking opportunities where I can contribute to hardware development, embedded firmware, and electronic systems engineering.
